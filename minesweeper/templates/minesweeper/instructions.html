How to Play Minesweeper

Objective: The goal of Minesweeper is to clear a rectangular board containing hidden "mines" without detonating any of them.

Game Rules:

  • Left-Click: Reveal a tile. If it's a mine, the game ends. If it's safe, a number will appear indicating how many mines are adjacent to that tile.
  • Right-Click: Flag a tile you suspect contains a mine. This helps you keep track of potential mines.
  • Numbers: The numbers on the tiles indicate how many mines are in the adjacent tiles (up, down, left, right, and diagonally).
  • Winning: You win the game by revealing all non-mine tiles without clicking on any mines.

Tips:

  • Start by clicking on a tile in the middle of the board. This gives you the best chance of opening up a large area.
  • Use the numbers to deduce where mines are located. If a tile shows "1" and there is only one adjacent unrevealed tile, that tile must be a mine.
  • Flag tiles you are certain contain mines to avoid accidentally clicking on them.

Note: Minesweeper is a game of logic and patience. Take your time to analyze the board and make informed decisions.
